Welcome to the OpenWhisk Shell

The OpenWhisk Shell is a research prototype, aiming to raise the abstraction level of commands, and enrich the valuable CLI experience with graphics where it really helps. Though a prototype, it is robust, and currently backed by over 600 tests.

In order to help us assess the tool, we need your help! For a period of time, we need to collect usage information. Specifically, we will count the commands you execute, but not the arguments used for those commands. For example, we will know that you created an action via the let command, but not the name of the action, nor its body.
